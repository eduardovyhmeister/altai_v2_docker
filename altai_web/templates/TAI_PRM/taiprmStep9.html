Percentage of each failure condition

The Risk Priority Number index (RPN) with respect to a concrete failure mode enables the normalisation of the AI artefacts risks. The formula to calculate it is in Equation the following equation, corresponding to the multiplication of three indexes: severity, likelihood and detection ranking

RPN = S*O*D



The higher the RPN is, the higher the risk involved in the analysed failure mode for that AI component, therefore the item or component can be identified as a source of a failing condition with different degrees of impact. Furthermore, to evaluate the global risk of an AI artifact on different failure modes (GRPN_item - Global Risk Priority Number index), it is needed to perform the sum of the RPN of each item $i$ to its corresponding failure mode ratio as proposed in the following equation:

GRPN = ∑ RPNi* αi



Where i represents the different failure mode linked to the same source; n is the number of failure modes of an specific component; and α_i is the failure mode ratio and represents the part attributed to a concrete failure mode if the failure materializes. This means the percentage of the AI artifact to fail with an specific failure mode.
