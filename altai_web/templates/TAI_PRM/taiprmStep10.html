Risk Register

The risk register is an instrument used for recording the risk management process dedicated to identifying risks'. Its purpose is to facilitate ownership and management of each risk. At the same time, it is used to generate a record of the risks that have been identified. Furthermore, it serves as a record of the control activities that are currently undertaken or will be used to improve the control of the particular risk. The risk register will cover the significant risks facing the organisation/project and record the results of the system risk assessment.
